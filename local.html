Local Information

Conference events will be located at 1925 N 12th Street, Philadelphia, PA 19122, in SERC Room 110A (the Science Education and Research Center).

For people flying into PHL airport, there is a convenient regional rail line right outside the airport (follow signs) that connects directly to Temple University campus.

If you are taking Amtrak to get here, we recommend using SEPTA subways (the blue L line and then the orange B line) or the buses.
