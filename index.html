Section 1 Fundamental group

Definition 1.1.
Let \(X\) be a topological space. The fundamental group of \(X\) at basepoint \(x_0\) is the set of equivalence classes of loops through \(x_0\) up to homotopy, with the group operation \(\cdot\) concatenation.
We will denote the fundamental group of a topological space \(X\) with basepoint \(x_0\) as defined by Definition 1.1 as \(\pi_1(X, x_0) \text{.}\)

Theorem 1.2.

The fundamental group is a group.
